Description

Save The Lab is a PVP Action Puzzler in which you need quick thinking and fast reflexes to win.

Story

You are a student in a lab and your professor had an accident. His potions are about to explode and you need to add ingredients quickly to stabilize them. Whoever manages to add 10 ingredients first while keeping the potions stable wins.

Gameplay and Features

In Save The Lab you compete against your opponent locally on the same keyboard. Each player needs to take ingredients from the middle of the screen by inputting the arrows shown on screen. The first player to collect 10 ingredients first wins. However, there are two scales that get increased or decreased depending on the shape and color of the taken ingredient. You need to analyze the ingredients instead of taking them blindly and balance the scales or otherwise your potion explodes and you lose.
