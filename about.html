About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio. My name is Marc Riewe and I am a game developer from Germany, specialising in Programming, Game Design and QA. I combine technical experience and knowledge in game design to help create fun and robust gameplay. Together with my expertise in Game Design and QA I am able to view requirements from different angles and develop them not only the way they are asked, but the way they make sense and are fun. This is further aided by my logical, strategic and planning approach and motivation to keep on learning new things.

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree in Game Design at IU International University in Cologne, Germany. My goal is to learn as much as possible about game development as possible to be able to create innovative gameplay experiences.

Before starting my current studies I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Business Informatics at EUFH, which is now part of CBS. During and after this degree I worked in software testing and test automation as part of agile teams for almost 5 years. This equipped me well for working in software development and shaped my interest in Programming and QA. I bring hands-on experience in software development and testing.

You can contact me on LinkedIn or at my E-Mail. I would love to connect and talk about game projects or just games in general.
